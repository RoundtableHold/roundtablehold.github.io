Limgrave

  • Name
    Location
  • Name: Banished Knight Engvall Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Grave Warden Duelist boss in Murkwater Catacombs.
  • Name: Banished Knight Oleg Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Ulcerated Tree Spirit boss in Fringefolk Hero's Grave.
  • Name: Godrick Soldier Ashes
    Location: Found in a cemetery filled with Spirit Jellyfish at the west end of Stormhill.
  • Name: Lone Wolf Ashes
    Location: Given by Renna at the Church of Elleh after aquiring Torrent from Melina.
  • Name: Noble Sorcerer Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Erdtree Burial Watchdog boss in the Stormfoot Catacombs.
  • Name: Skeletal Militiaman Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Tibia Mariner field boss at Summonwater Village.
  • Name: Spirit Jellyfish Ashes
    Location: Given by Roderika in Stormhill Shack after talking to her several times.
  • Name: Stormhawk Deenh
    Location: Found in a chest above the main hall of the Chapel of Anticipation, after returning there via The Four Belfries.
  • Name: Wandering Noble Ashes
    Location: Found on a corpse resting against a coffin, up a ladder and guarded by some Jade Goblins in the Stormfoot Catacombs.

  • Name
    Location
  • Name: Albinauric Ashes
    Location: Found on a corpse in the graveyard area east of Caria Manor.
  • Name: Avionette Soldier Ashes
    Location: Found on a corpse in Raya Lucaria Academy, down the rotating lift on the ledge before reaching the bottom.
  • Name: Black Knife Tiche Ashes
    Location: Dropped by Alecto, Black Knife Ringleader in the Ringleader's Evergaol.
  • Name: Dolores the Sleeping Arrow Puppet
    Location: First sold by Preceptor Seluvis in Seluvis's Rise, then later can be found on the body of Pidia in Caria Manor.
  • Name: Dung Eater Puppet
    Location: Sold by Preceptor Seluvis in Seluvis's Rise after using Seluvis's Potion on the Dung Eater.
  • Name: Fanged Imp Ashes
    Location: Can be selected as a Keepsake; otherwise, sold by the Isolated Merchant east of the Main Academy Gate site of grace.
  • Name: Finger Maiden Therolina Puppet
    Location: Sold by Preceptor Seluvis's body in Seluvis's Rise after completing Ranni's questline.
  • Name: Glintstone Sorcerer Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Spirit-Caller Snail boss in the Road's End Catacombs.
  • Name: Jarwight Puppet
    Location: Sold by Preceptor Seluvis's body in Seluvis's Rise after completing Ranni's questline.
  • Name: Kaiden Sellsword Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Erdtree Burial Watchdog boss in the Impaler's Catacombs.
  • Name: Land Squirt Ashes
    Location: Found at a grave surrounded by land squirts on the east shore of the lake, north of the Gate Town Bridge site of grace.
  • Name: Latenna the Albinauric
    Location: Found at the Slumbering Wolf's Shack, out the end of Lakeside Crystal Cave. You must possess the Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) for her to join you.
  • Name: Marionette Soldier Ashes
    Location: Found on a corpse hanging off the edge of the lower path past the bridge in the graveyard in Raya Lucaria Academy.
  • Name: Nepheli Loux Puppet
    Location: First sold by Preceptor Seluvis in Seluvis's Rise after using Seluvis's Potion on Nepheli Loux, then later can be found on the body of Pidia in Caria Manor.
  • Name: Page Ashes
    Location: Found on a tomb being watched over by an Ogre in the final room of the Cliffbottom Catacombs.
  • Name: Raya Lucaria Soldier Ashes
    Location: Found behind an illusory wall at the end of the Road's End Catacombs.
  • Name: Skeletal Bandit Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Tibia Mariner field boss, south of the Carian Study Hall.
  • Name: Twinsage Sorcerer Ashes
    Location: Dropped by the Cemetery Shade boss in the Black Knife Catacombs.
