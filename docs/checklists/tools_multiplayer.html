Functional

  • Name
    Description
    Location
  • Name: Memory of Grace
    Description: Return to last Site of Grace; destroys currently held runes, but leaves intact existing rune retrieval
    Location: Granted automatically at start of game
  • Name: Telescope
    Description: Shift camera into first person view
    Location: Bought from Kalé in Church of Elleh, Limgrave, for 500 runes
  • Name: Spectral Steed Whistle
    Description: Summon or dismiss Torrent
    Location: Obtained by meeting Melina at the Gatefront Ruins Site of Grace in Limgrave
  • Name: Mimic's Veil
    Description: Spend 6 FP to disguise self as an inanimate object
    Location: Stormveil Castle: Up the stairs from the room with the Grafted Scion, in the room just past the dropoff for the Chrysalid's Memento, in a chest guarded by a Banished Knight
  • Name: Lantern
    Description: Dimly illuminate surroundings
    Location: Bought for 1800 runes from either the Isolated Merchant at the Isolated Merchant's Shack Site of Grace in Weeping Peninsula or the Nomadic Merchant by the Liurnia Lake Shore Site of Grace in Liurnia
  • Name: Pureblood Knight's Medal
    Description: Teleport self to Dynasty Mausoleum Entrance Site of Grace; can be used to warp out of any combat safely
    Location: Given by Varré at Rose Church, Liurnia, at the end of his various requests

Combat

  • Name
    Description
    Location
  • Name: Margit's Shackle
    Description: Usable up to twice in each attempt at the first phase of Margit, miniboss Margit, or Morgott: stun them for a few seconds
    Location: Bought from Patches for 5000 runes
  • Name: Wraith Calling Bell
    Description: Spend 7 FP to conjure homing projectiles
    Location: In northwest Laskyar Ruins, Liurnia of the Lakes, in the underground chest
  • Name: Ancestral Infant's Head
    Description: Spend 27 FP to spawn geysers that deal magic damage to enemies
    Location: In the submerged ruin in northwestern Ancestral Woods in Nokron, Eternal City; carefully platform to the far side and defeat the Ancestral Follower guarding a chest with this tool
  • Name: Mohg's Shackle
    Description: Usable up to twice in each attempt at the first phase of Mohg: stun him for a few seconds
    Location: Found in Subterranean Shunning-Grounds guarded by two Giant Crayfish
  • Name: Omen Bairn
    Description: Spend 24 FP to conjure wraiths that chase enemies
    Location: From the Underground Roadside Site of Grace in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds, with shortcuts unlocked, go through the first two doors then down the ladder, and defeat an Omen enemy who drops it
  • Name: Regal Omen Bairn
    Description: Spend 50 FP to conjure more wraiths that deal more damage
    Location: One of the choices offered when trading in the Remembrance of the Omen King to Finger Reader Enia at the Roundtable Hold
  • Name: Blasphemous Claw
    Description: Parry specific melee attacks by Maliketh, stunning him
    Location: Dropped by Recusant Bernahl in Crumbling Farum Azula

Multiplayer

  • Name
    Description
    Location
  • Name: Tarnished's Wizened Finger
    Description: Write messages in online play
    Location: Found directly in the beginning of the game, on a body in the building you spawn into
  • Name: Tarnished's Furled Finger
    Description: Place your cooperative summon sign in online play
    Location: In the room with the Stranded Graveyard Site of Grace on a body
  • Name: Finger Severer
    Description: In online play, return to your world, or send your co-op phantoms home
    Location: On the same body as the previous
  • Name: Small Golden Effigy
    Description: Send your cooperative summon sign to all nearby activated Martyr Effigies at once in online play
    Location: Found on the first Martyr Effigy, nearby The First Step Site of Grace
  • Name: Furlcalling Finger Remedy
    Description: In online play, view summon signs placed by other players, whether cooperative or competitive; item is refunded unless you successfully summon someone
    Location: Technically not a tool as it's a consumable, but easily craftable with two Erdleaf Flowers, dropped by invaders including many NPCs, and a frequent loot pickup or shop item.
  • Name: Festering Bloody Finger
    Description: Invade another player as a Bloody Finger in online play
    Location: Technically not a tool as it's a consumable, but can be bought in limited quantities from the Nomadic Merchant of East Limgrave, Patches, and the Imprisoned Merchant, and infinitely from the Hermit Merchant in Leyndell. Varré gives you five during his questline.
  • Name: Phantom Bloody Finger
    Description: As an invader in online play, re-attempt your current invasion; useful if you can't find the host
    Location: You're automatically granted three of these consumables if you invade someone, and you lose them once the invasion has concluded
  • Name: Duelist's Furled Finger
    Description: Place your competitive summon sign in online play
    Location: At the northern tip of Limgrave at the foot of the ruined round structure, directly next to the ghost, near where Recusant Henricus invades
  • Name: Small Red Effigy
    Description: Send your competitive summon sign to all nearby activated Martyr Effigies at once in online play
    Location: On the same body as the previous
  • Name: Taunter's Tongue
    Description: Allow your world to be invaded without cooperators present, and for a second invader to join if you have a cooperator. Requires using a Furlcalling Finger Remedy first.
    Location: Dropped by NPC invader Mad Tongue Alberich in Roundtable Hold, down off the balcony
  • Name: White Cipher Ring
    Description: When invaded in online play, summon someone using the Blue Cipher Ring to aid you as a hunter; stays active permanently until you turn it off
    Location: Bought from Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold for 1000 runes
  • Name: Blue Cipher Ring
    Description: Be summoned as a hunter anytime someone nearby using the White Cipher Ring is invaded; stays active permanently until you turn it off
    Location: Bought from Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold for 1000 runes
  • Name: Bloody Finger
    Description: Invade another player as a Bloody Finger in online play; an infinite Festering Bloody Finger that replaces them in the Multiplayer menu
    Location: Given by Varré at Rose Church, Liurnia, at the end of his various requests
  • Name: Recusant Finger
    Description: Invade another player as a Recusant Finger in online play; nearly identical to Bloody Finger, though biases players who've used the Taunter's Tongue
    Location: Found on the table with Requests in Volcano Manor
  • Name: Phantom Great Rune
    Description: When an invader in online play, buffs nearby enemies with the "Blessing of Blood"
    Location: You're automatically granted three of these consumables if you invade someone after using a Rune Arc with Mohg's Great Rune equipped, and you lose them once the invasion has concluded
