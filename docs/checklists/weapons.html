Daggers

  • Name
    Location
  • Name: Dagger
    Location: Purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold.
  • Name: Parrying Dagger
    Location: Purchased from Patches inside Murkwater Cave after he sets up shop or if killed, from using his bell bearing.
  • Name: Misericorde
    Location: Found on a corpse inside the Armory (Requires a Stonesword Key to enter). [Map Link]
  • Name: Great Knife
    Location: Starting Equipment for the Bandit Class. 2% drop chance from Demi-Humans wielding this weapon, such as those in Lakeside Crystal Cave.
  • Name: Bloodstained Dagger
    Location: 4% drop chance from Demi-Human Chiefs.
  • Name: Erdsteel Dagger
    Location: Rewarded by Kenneth Haight for clearing out Fort Haight.
  • Name: Wakizashi
    Location: Gaol Cave: Found inside a locked cell (unlocked via a lever).
  • Name: Celebrant's Sickle
    Location: 0.5% drop chance from the blue-hooded Celebrants in Windmill Village.
  • Name: Ivory Sickle
    Location: Found on a stone coffin next to Albus at the Village of the Albinaurics.
  • Name: Crystal Knife
    Location: Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel: Found inside a chest on one of the lower levels.
  • Name: Scorpion's Stinger
    Location: Located inside a chest at the end of the brazier-lit Grand Cloister; guarded by many Lesser Kindred of Rot (Pests) enemies.
  • Name: Cinquedea
    Location: Found at the bottom of a long series of drop-downs near the south-west side of Bestial Sanctum. Starting at south-western cliff, look for a thick tree root growing into the cliffside to drop down to.
  • Name: Glintstone Kris
    Location: Rewarded for siding with Sorceress Sellen at the end of her questline.
  • Name: Reduvia
    Location: Dropped from the invading NPC Bloody Finger Nerijus; just outside Murkwater Cave. *IMPORTANT NOTE* Do not let Bloody Finger Hunter Yura die during this invasion if you wish to continue his questline.
  • Name: Blade of Calling
    Location: While riding up the lift found behind the Forbidden Land's site of grace, face south-west to find a room built into the wall.
  • Name: Black Knife
    Location: Dropped from the Black Knife Assassin guarding the entrance of Sainted Hero's Grave.

Straight Swords

  • Name
    Location
  • Name: Short Sword
    Location: Starting Equipment for the Astrologer Class. Purchased from the Nomadic Merchant across the Saintsbridge, in North Limgrave. [Map Link]
  • Name: Longsword
    Location: Starting Equipment for the Vagabond Class. Purchased from Twin Maiden Husks at the Roundtable Hold.
  • Name: Broadsword
    Location: Starting Equipment for the Confessor Class. Purchased from the Nomadic Merchant just south-east of Coastal Cave. [Map Link]
  • Name: Weathered Straight Sword
    Location: 2% drop chance from Commoner enemies.
  • Name: Lordsworn's Straight Sword
    Location: 2% drop chance from Godrick Soldiers found all over Limgrave. A good place to find many of these enemies is Gatefront Ruins.
  • Name: Noble's Slender Sword
    Location: 0.5% drop chance from Common Nobles wielding this weapon. Notable farming areas include the graveyard south of the Caelid Highway South grace site and Raya Lucaria Academy - just outside the Debate Parlor grace.
  • Name: Cane Sword
    Location: Leyndell, Royal Capital: Start from the West Capital Ramparts and head south, taking the first set of stairs you see. At the bottom of the stairs, immediately hang right and turn 180 to find a corpse with the sword.
  • Name: Warhawk's Talon
    Location: 2% drop chance from Bladed Talon Eagles found in Stormveil Castle. A good spot is just north of the Rampart Tower grace site.
  • Name: Lazuli Glintsone Sword
    Location: 2% drop chance from Lazuli Sorcerers found in Raya Lucaria Academy and Caria Manor.
  • Name: Carian Knight's Sword
    Location: Found in the north-western area of Liurnia of the Lakes inside a chest on the back of troll-drawn carriage. [Map Link] *Note* Hitting one of the trolls pulling the carriage will cause them to stop moving.
  • Name: Crystal Sword
    Location: Found at the Village of the Albinaurics. From the grace site, go north across the bridge to find the sword on a corpse near the cliffedge.
  • Name: Rotten Crystal Sword
    Location: Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree: From the Elphael Inner Wall grace site, head south down the stairs and climb over the tree roots on your left. The sword is looted from a chest guarded by three Crystalians.
  • Name: Miquellan Knight's Sword
    Location: Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree: At the top of the bell tower [Map Link]. From the Prayer Room grace site, head north along the walkway until you reach a stone basin with a small walkway on your right. At the end of the small walkway, drop down to the bell tower's ledge and carefully walk around to the eastern side of the tower where you will find a ladder. At the top of the ladder will be the sword.
  • Name: Ornamental Straight Sword
    Location: Dropped from the Grafted Scion at the Chapel of Anticipation. Players can return to this area after the tutorial by using the waygate (denoted by the message 'Precipice of Anticipation') [Map Link] at The Four Belfries. (Requires an Imbued Sword Key)
  • Name: Golden Epitaph
    Location: Auriza Hero's Grave in the eastern Capital Outskirts: Looted from a corpse found behind the Imp Statue's fog wall near the grace site. (Requires a Stonesword Key to enter)
  • Name: Sword of St Trina
    Location: Found down an imp sealed staircase in the Forsaken Ruins, just below the Rotview Balcony grace site. (Requires a Stonesword Key to enter)
  • Name: Regalia of Eochaid
    Location: Found on a cliffside at the end of Gaol Cave. Can also be accessed by dropping down from the cliff above to skip the cave. [Map Link]
  • Name: Coded Sword
    Location: Leyndell, Royal Capital: From the West Capital Ramparts head south and find a safe way down somewhere to your right. Directly across from a large tree root will be partially sunken horse stable, climb onto its roof and head forward to enter the building. Keep making your way inward until you come across a broken roundtable. There will be a set of double doors, open them and the sword will be waiting for you on a throne. [Map Link]
  • Name: Sword of Night and Flame
    Location: Caria Manor: From the Manor Lower Level grace site, head south-east across the rooftop walls (mind the blue spectral soldiers), making a left (north) at the first intersection, follow the pathway until it ends. There will be a rooftop to the north, run and jump to safely make it. To the north, beyond a small broken wall, will be another lower rooftop with a ladder, climb down to find a chest with the sword inside. [Map Link]

  • Name: Sword of Milos
    Location: Dung Eater's sword; drops if killed in the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds sewer-goal (requires the Sewer-Gaol Key) or by defeating him as an invader in the Capital Outskirts moat. [Map Link] Both options require progression of his questline.

    Location: Dropped from Elemer of the Briar at the end of The Shaded Castle.
  • Name: Ordovis's Greatsword
    Location: Dropped from Crucible Knight Ordovis at the end of Auriza Hero's Grave. *NOTE* You will have to fight both Ordovis (sword and shield) and a spear-wielding Crucible Knight at the same time!
  • Name: Alabaster Lord's Sword
    Location: Dropped from the Alabaster Lord at the Lake of Rot. Requires standing on two different pressure plates; one to raise the stone column walkway and the other to lift you up onto the walkway. [Map Link] These pressure plates can be found accompanied by a stone obelisk throughout the lake.

    Location: Dropped from a Death Rite Bird (only spawns at night!) in Caelid, just south-east of the Southern Aeonia Swamp Bank grace site. [Map Link]
  • Name: Helphen's Steeple

  • Name: Blasphemous Blade
    Location: One of the choices offered when trading in the Remembrance of the Blasphemous to Finger Reader Enia at the Roundtable Hold.
  • Name: Golden Order Greatsword
    Location: Dropped from Misbegotten Crusader at the end of the Cave of the Forlorn in Consecrated Snowfield.
  • Name: Dark Moon Greatsword
    Location: Rewarded from Ranni the Witch underneath the Cathedral of Manus Celes at the end of her questline.
  • Name: Sacred Relic Sword

    Location: On a body located at the bottom of Highroad Cave, on a platform after the waterfall section, being feasted on by Giant Bats. [Map Link]
  • Name: Grossmesser
    Location: 2% drop chance from Skeletal Militiamen wielding this weapon in Tombsward Catacombs.
  • Name: Bandit's Curved Sword
    Location: 2% drop chance from Skeletal Bandits, such as those near Church of Pilgrimage and Hermit Merchant's Shack.
  • Name: Shotel
    Location: Purchased from the Nomadic Merchant in Siofra River for 2500 Runes. [Map Link]
  • Name: Scavenger's Curved Sword
    Location: Found in Mt. Gelmir, on a corpse near a Grafted Scion across a stone pillar-bridge north west of the First Mt. Gelmir Campsite site of grace. The corpse closest to the ladder has the blade. [Map Link]
  • Name: Mantis Blade
    Location: Dropped by Cemetery Shade in Gelmir Hero's Grave. [Map Link]
  • Name: Beastman's Curved Sword
    Location: 2% drop chance from Azula Beastmen carrying this weapon. 7 beastmen carrying this weapon can be found near the Crumbling Beast Grave Site of Grace in Crumbling Farum Azula. Turn right after leaving the room with the grace, the beastmen will be in two rooms ahead. Four of the beastmen are eating and do not respond unless the player attacks or walks near their head.
  • Name: Flowing Curved Sword
    Location: Consecrated Snowfield: Southwest of the Inner Consecrated Snowfield Site of Grace a caravan is pulling a Hearse with a chest containing the Flowing Curved Sword. The Hearse is protected at night by a double Night's Cavalry boss fight. [Map Link]
  • Name: Serpent-God's Curved Sword
    Location: Can be found at Ruin-Strewn Precipice. From the Ruin-Strewn Precipice Overlook Grace, take the elevator down to end up behind the two singing bats. Traversing northward takes you to a ladder, and the sword is behind the Land Octopus. [Map Link]
  • Name: Magma Blade
    Location: Rare drop from Man-Serpents wielding this weapon at Volcano Manor.
  • Name: Nox Flowing Sword
    Location: Sellia, Town of Sorcery: Dropped by the Nox Swordstress & Nox Priest boss duo upon defeat. [Map Link]
  • Name: Wing of Astel
    Location: Found in a small chest at the top of the ruins in southern Uhl Palace Ruins. Can only be reached if you have access to the northern section of Ainsel River, by following the river east from northern Uhl Palace Ruins. From the Nokstella, Eternal City Site of Grace, look east at the waterfall and go forward, climbing up the ladder you see on your right. Go through the door and up the watery path, hugging the right wall, you'll encounter an ant enemy along the way and through the tunnel you'll see a T-Posing enemy with a scythe at the cliff. Continue hugging the right wall, and drop down safely down the ledges and up the path where the chest is located on the walkway behind the Malformed Star enemy. Be careful of the spells it fires at you, so you don't get knocked off the walkway. [Map Link]
  • Name: Eclipse Shotel
    Location: Castle Sol: Found at the altar of the church in the southeast section of the castle. [Map Link]
