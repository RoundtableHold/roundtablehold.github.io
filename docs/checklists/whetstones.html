    Notes: Found on a corpse slumped in front of an altar in Night's Sacred Ground
  • Effect: Sanctified Whetblade
    Location: Leyndell, Royal Capital
    Notes: Found in the Fortified Manor, accessible from the Avenue Balcony Grace
